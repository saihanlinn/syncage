Our Mission

The Vision

To create a global economy where every transaction restores the planet and accelerates human prosperity, making verified impact the standard for every purchase.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional charity relies on "donor dependency." Syncage moves NGOs toward "revenue independence" through sustainable commerce and API-first integration.

The Solution

A data-driven marketplace that translates purpose-driven products into measurable UN SDG contributions, enabling consumers to build a professional legacy of positive change.

The Seller Journey: API-First Revenue Independence

Connect your warehouse systems directly to Syncage. No manual posting. Real-time inventory sync, standardized impact calculation, and access to a global marketplace.

  • API-First Integration: Connect existing warehouse systems directly
  • Inventory Sync: Real-time stock levels across local and global systems
  • Standardized Impact Builder: Map field data to UN SDGs and IRIS+ coefficients
  • Automated Data Push/Pull: Sync with accounting software automatically
  • Zero Manual Burden: Fully automated operational workflow

The Platform Brain: The Hub Infrastructure

Syncage acts as the world's central clearing house for social enterprise data. Open APIs connect logistics, corporate ESG dashboards, and professional networks.

  • Shipment Integration: Real-time tracking from DHL, NinjaVan, local couriers
  • Third-Party APIs: Corporate ESG dashboards pull verified impact data
  • Unified Data Standard: Consistent JSON format for global scalability
  • Audit-Ready Metrics: All transactions mapped to verified standards
  • Standard-Setter Role: Defining the 2030 Agenda for impact commerce
